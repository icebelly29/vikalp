Housing, Infrastructure and Water Purification

Housing

Central Building Research Institute (CBRI), Roorkee and Structural Engineering Research Centre (SERC), Madras, have done a great deal of work in these areas.

Infrastructure

Integrated Rural Accessibility Planning (IRAP) simultaneously seeks to improve the rural transport system and distribution of facilities and services. The objective of the process is to - in a cost effective way - improve access to goods and services in rural areas, in particular for poor and disadvantaged communities

Water Purification

Community participation, technology transfer, evaluation and assessment of existing rural water supply systems, studies on maintenance of water distribution system, development and rehabilitation of springs and use of hydraulic rams for water supply in hilly areas, use of solar energy for rural water supply, development of integrated package for water treatment, to make the water potable; etc.

Our main goal with regards to Housing would be to direct those who need it to the CBRI and SERC.
